# Entry, Exit & Long-Run Profitability

A slide-based lecture primer on market structures, entry and exit dynamics, and long-run profitability. Covers economic profit, the U-shaped average cost curve, how free entry and exit drive economic profits to zero, and the strategies firms use to sustain profits through barriers to entry. Designed for introductory and intermediate economics and for general readers who want a grounded treatment of why markets are competitive and what protects firms from that competition.

## View the presentation

The slides are live at: [https://brendanjameshart.github.io/pc-market-structures/](https://brendanjameshart.github.io/pc-market-structures/)

You can also view them locally by opening `index.html` in any web browser.

## What is inside

`index.html` is the complete rendered slide presentation, self-contained and viewable in any modern browser with an internet connection. The file includes 34 slides and speaker notes, with interactive Chart.js diagrams for the average cost curve, profit margin, entry and exit progressions, three-case equilibrium analysis, and cost advantage diagrams.
